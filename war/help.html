My Profiles

What is a Profile?
A Profile is used to help manage, organize, store and search your photos. This is the first step in creating a person’s photo-biography. You should create Profiles for family and friends that are part of your everyday life, the people that you take photos of on a regular basis. (i.e. you are helping them to create a photo documentary of their lives) For other people that you take photos of throughout the year that are not as involved in your everyday life myStoredLife created an Events category for storing these photos called Other Family and Friends. As an example, you would create Profiles for your children, spouse, parents, brothers and sisters, good friends and their children; if you are actively involved in their lives (you attend their birthday parties, school or sporting events, spend holidays or vacations together, etc…). For everyone else, you would store their photos under the person’s Profile they most relate to and then under the Event "Other Family and Friends" with a sub-event created in their name.
Are there any limitations on how many Profiles I can create?
You can create as many Profiles as you want. The key to using myStoredLife most effectively is to only create Profiles for people where you are active in each other’s lives. Everyone else will be stored and managed through the Event "Other Family and Friends".
How do I add a new Profile?
Just click on "Add New Profile" which opens a "Create Profile" dialog box. Fill in all details and enter create. Refresh "My Profiles" section and you’ll see the newly created Profile.
What happens when I create a new Profile?
As soon as you create a new Profile the person’s name will appear on the "My Profile section" without Profile picture. Also their name will show up in the "STORE", "SEARCH" and "PHOTO SHARING" areas to allow you to store, search and share the images that are related to them.
How do I add a new Profile picture? For ex: My son?
You need to store at least one image of your son before populating Profile picture. Follow the steps below:
  1. Go to Upload and upload an image of your son. Uploaded images will show up in Work area.
  2. Go to Work Area and add "Photo Info" such as Caption, Who and Date", save it.
  3. Click on "Store Photos" and store the image by selecting your Son’s Profile.
  4. Go back to "My Profiles" and click on "Change Photo" underneath your son’s Profile, which opens a "Search Profile Photo" window
  5. Search for your son’s image by entering one of the attributes from the previously added image or simple click search. Select the image you want to use for your Son’s Profile photo and follow the next steps on the screen
How can I edit/change a Profile?
  1. Go to "My Profiles" and locate your son’s Profile
  2. Click on "Edit", make necessary changes and save it back.
How can I delete my son’s Profile?
  1. Go to "My Profiles and locate your son’s Profile
  2. Click on "Delete" and follow steps
What happens to all the images if I delete my son’s Profile?
You lose all the images as soon as you delete any Profile. Take extra precautions while performing this action. If you want to keep the images before deleting any Profile:
  1. Go To "Search"
  2. Search for all images of your son by selecting "your son’s Profile"
  3. Display results show all the images of your son.
  4. Select all the images that you want to keep by clicking on the "To Work Area" icon. This will push the images to "Work Area"
  5. Later you can delete the Profile, but photos are stored in the "Work Area" for future usage.

Upload

How do I upload my Images?
Click on the “Upload” button and follow the given instructions.
There are 2 upload options available:
  1. Simple Upload: Allows you to upload a single image at a time and the size of the image must be less than 1 MB.
  2. Batch Upload: Allows you to upload more than one image, Use the browser window to locate your pictures in your computer’s directory using the Java console, drag and drop or double click the images to place them in the upload window and click on the Start Upload icon
Is there any limitation on the Image size that I upload?
No, there is not a limitation on the image size. myStoredLife reduces the image size to 200-300kB (1600 X 1200 pixels) to increase upload speed and optimize your viewing experience.
Is there a way to delete an image that I drag and drop in the console window before start uploading?
Yes, that’s very simple. Every image added to the upload window using Java Console will have few icons displayed with that image. To delete an image before uploading click on the mark for that image and it will be removed from the upload window. See image below.
Can I edit an image before start uploading?
Any image can be edited prior to uploading the image. To edit the image, click on the “Edit image” icon . The editing tool enables you to do simple editing such as Rotate, Crop, Resize, etc. For advanced editing we suggest using “Google Picnic Editor” which is integrated with myStoredLife.
If I upload an image now when will it be displayed?
The moment your image is uploaded, images will show up in the "Work Area".

What does “Store Photo” button do?
This enables you to store images using the myStoredLife photo organizational structure. You store photos in a person’s Profile and with Events and Sub Events to make it easier to search and share photos in the future. When you click on “Store Photo” you first select one or more people’s Profiles, then the Event that best represents the photo and finally you can create and name Sub Events to help organize your photos. You can store photos to any level you want. As an example, you can store a photo at the Profile level only, or select an Event under the Profile and finally you can create and name a Sub Event as the final level of storage. The more levels you use to store your photos the more organized they will be and the more specific you can search them in the future. Search

What is Search?
The Search area enables user to search their stored images based on one or multiple search criteria (i.e. Profile Name, Caption, Who, Date, Event, etc.). If you create a search with a large search result only the first 50 images of the search will be displayed. Click on the link at the bottom of the displayed images to load the next 50 photos.
What search criteria is available?
User can search by one or more criteria’s such as “Caption, Who, Date, Profile Name, Events, etc”
  • Search By Caption - Enables you to search for any image by exact caption (tag) used while storing images in the Work Area. For ex: Reunion at Central Park, Jimmy’s Band Concert, etc. Any the images tagged with any part of the above captions will be displayed.
  • Search By Who - Enables you to search for any image by the name of the person tagged in the image. For ex: If you tag Jenny Smith under “Add Photo Info - Who” in the Work area, then you can search Jenny Smith under the “Who” criteria in Search and all images tagged with Jenny Smith will be displayed.
  • Search By When - Enables user to search for any image by exact date or variations of dates stored with an image in Work Area. For ex: If you have stored an image in the Work Area with the date 10/22/2002, then when you search by specific date “10/22/2002” all the images stored under this date will be displayed. Note: Date set on the actual image in the Work Area will show up, not the date image was uploaded. myStoredLife provides many other date search options such as search by “Last 7 Days”, “Months To Date”, “Year To Date”, “The Previous Month”, “Specific Date”, “All Dates Before”, “All Dates After” and “Date Range”.
  • Search By Profiles - Enables you to search images by Profile name. If you only search by the Profile name the last 50 images stored under that profile will be displayed. Click on the link at the bottom of the displayed images to load the next 50 photos.
  • Search By Events - Enables you to search for an image by the Event it was stored under. For ex: if you saved an image under the “Birthday” Event with a Sub Event called “2nd”, you can create a search by selecting Event “Birthday” then Sub Event “2nd” and all the images stored under Event “Birthday” Sub Event “2nd” will be displayed.
What is the expected behavior when you search only on top level Event?
The expected behavior is this: When you search for images based on the "Event" only, the search result will be all the images stored under the “Event” and all associated “Sub Event” categories. When you search for images where the “Event” and “Sub Event” is selected only the images in that “Sub Event” will be displayed.

Photo Sharing

What is Photo Sharing?
Photo Sharing is an easy way to share photos with other myStoredLife users, as well as, people not in the myStoredLife family and other technologies like Facebook and Google Buzz.
Under "Photo Collections" you see two different tabs as shown below:
My Collections:
There are 2 types of collection under "My Collection" tab:
  1. myStoredLife automatically creates a collection for every Profile person you create. This collection will be displayed under the "My collections" tab as a Profile Collection
  2. You can also create a custom collection by manually searching and adding photos of your choice. This collection will be displayed under "My collections" tab as Custom Collection.
How can I add photos to "Auto collection" or "Profile Collection"?
Every time you save a photo under a person’s Profile it is send to their Profile Collection, unless you mark the photo as private.
Can I share "Auto collection" or "Profile Collection" with everyone?
Profile collections can only be shared with other myStoredLife users.
To share a collection with other myStoredLife users, click on "Share Collections" and search for the name of the myStoredLife user (for ex: John Smith). Once you find the right person in the search window, select that person by double clicking on the image and click on "Share" button. All photos sent to a Profile Collection will now be shared with the myStoredLife users you selected and show up in their "Received Collections"
How do I create Custom Collection?
  1. Go to Search Page and search for photos of your choice
  2. From search result window select the photos you want to share.
  3. Click on "Create Collection" button.
  4. Enter the appropriate name for the collection and save it.
  5. If you already have existing collection, just add these photos by clicking on "Add To Collection" button.
    6. These collections will show up under "Custom Collection" section of "My Collections" tab displaying actual number of photos. These photos are now ready to share.
Can I share "Custom Collection" with everyone?
Yes! You can share custom collections with everybody from myStoredLife users, to Facebook, to Google Buzz. You can even share them using your Google Gmail account.
To share with myStoredLife users, just click on "Share Collections" and search for the name of the registered member (for ex: John Smith). Once you find the right person in the search window select that person by double clicking on the image and click on "Share" button.
Received Collections:
Received collections are the same as "My Collection", but the photos come from other users of myStoredLife.
What can I do with the Collections received from others?
  1. You can view those collections shared by other users. If you like any of those images, myStoredLife allows you to copy those photos to your work area where they can be managed/stored.
How can I store the photos shared by others?
  1. Click on the "Photo Sharing" button, then click on the "Received Collection" tab
  2. Click on the collection name and all photos stored under this collection will be displayed.
  3. Underneath every photo, there is a + symbol, click on it.
  4. Those images will be sent to your Work Area along with all the Captions, Who and Dates.
  5. If you prefer, you can modify the Captions, Who and Dates and store it as you wish.
How do I know that new photos have been added to my received collections?
  1. When new photos are sent to you in a collection the name of the collection is Bolded.

Favorites

What are Favorites?  
Favorites are used to bookmark photos quickly, so you can easily view and share them in the "Favorites" area in myStoredLife.com
How do I add images to my Favorites?
Any time you see this icon below an image, click on it and it will change to . This picture is now assigned to your favorites and will show up in your Favorites area. To remove the photo from your favorites simple click on the icon and it will change back to and be removed from your favorites.
Is there any limit to number of Favorites?  
There is no limit on the number of photos you can set as a favorite.

Time Capsule

What is a Time Capsule?
Time Capsules preserves a view of the things you like most at a particular snap shot in time. Using the list of favorites categories provided or creating your own favorites.
How do create a Time Capsule?
You create a Time Capsule for a specific Profile person by selecting the number of favorites you want to have in the Time Capsule.

Next you select the name of the person you want to create the Time Capsule for and the date you want it saved on and then click next.

Now it is time to add the favorites. Select a “Favorites” category from the pulldown menu or type your own favorite category in the “Create new category here” field. Then type the name of the favorite in the “My Favorite” field. Finally, click the “Add Photo” button to attach a picture to the favorite and then click next. If at any time you want to change your previous entry click the previous button.

Repeat this process for the remaining favorites. Now you are ready to view and share your Time Capsule.
Is there any limit to the number of Time Capsules I can create?
There is no limit on the number of Time Capsules you can create.
